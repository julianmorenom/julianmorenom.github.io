Albán

Multimedia performance piece written for seven musicians and performed live with three dancers and video. Albán is a performance that explores the roll of death as a starting place for creation. You can watch "Ausente" the fourth movement, as a virtual reality abstraction of the piece. This VR abstraction was exposed during I____AMERICA AND AMERICA_____ME, October 19, through Saturday, November 16 of 2019, at the SVA Flatiron Gallery, 133/141 West 21st Street, New York City. The music will be available on February 7 of 2020, through Hoy Records and Tambien.
